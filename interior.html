Headline

Our mission is to promote public safety and support the firefighting community by providing accurate, up-to-date information and resources, enforcing fire safety standards, and fostering collaboration between agencies and organizations. We are committed to protecting lives and property, and working tirelessly to prevent and mitigate the impacts of fires in our state.
