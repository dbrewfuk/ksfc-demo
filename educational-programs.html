Comprehensive Educational Programs

State Fire Rescue Training (SFRT) offers a wide range of educational programs designed for current and future first responders, as well as business, industrial, and local governments. With over 45 years of experience, SFRT prepares individuals and organizations to handle emergencies effectively.

Customized Training

Our quality programs are based on the latest recognized state and national standards. We can also customize the programs to suit the specific needs of your personnel.

Flexible Delivery

We understand the importance of convenience and accessibility. That's why we offer programs that can be scheduled and conducted at your location, providing training anytime, anywhere.

Experienced Faculty and Leading Facilities

At SFRT, we have highly experienced and capable adjunct faculty who bring real-world experience to our educational programs. Combined with one of the world's largest fleets of mobile training facilities and up-to-date curriculum materials, we provide the highest quality education for you and your organization.

Program Offerings

Our educational programs cover a wide range of subjects, including:

  • Initial certification programs
  • Re-certification / continuing education programs
  • Certificate, diploma, and degree programs
  • Fire Programs
  • Technical Rescue
  • Emergency Medical Programs, Including First Aid and CPR
  • Hazardous Materials
  • Specialized Subjects
  • Citizen and Community Preparedness and Prevention
  • Leadership / Management
  • National Fire Academy Programs
